Oh! Foodie

Web Design UI/UX | Front End Code

Figma - HTML5 - CSS3 - Sass - JavaScript - Bootstrap 5

The Brief

Oh! Foodie Is an innovative restaurant reservation service as well as a listing of events centered on your location.

It will provide an online platform for diners of all kinds who want to efficiently book their restaurant experience, it will also serve as a planner for those looking for an event or activity to do during special times. A website was required to explain the functionality of the product, encourage registrations and the roadmap to make it easy for users to book restaurants and organized activities.

I was hired to create the design of a dynamic website that has a good look with a modern and user-friendly appearance, I also developed all the views and designed the models of the templates for the internal pages as well as the user flow until the check-in. out. The brand had its identity manual so it was not necessary to touch its graphic identity at all, but they encouraged me to create design tips for the use of the website, benefiting the design and user experience to the maximum.
